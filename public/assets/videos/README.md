# Video Assets

## Place your watermarker video here

To add your video background:

1. Name your video file: `watermarker.mp4` (recommended) or `watermarker.webm`
2. Place it in this directory: `/public/assets/videos/`
3. The video will automatically display as the hero section background

### Recommended Video Specifications:
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264) or WebM
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (will loop automatically)
- **File Size**: Keep under 10MB for optimal loading
- **Compression**: Use moderate compression to balance quality and file size

### Tips:
- The video will automatically play, loop, and be muted
- A dark overlay is applied to ensure text readability
- The video is responsive and will cover the entire hero section
- Consider the video content - avoid busy scenes that might distract from the text
